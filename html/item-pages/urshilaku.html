Urshilaku Coat

Item Type

  • Common Shirt (not enchanted)

Description

  • The smooth malachite fragments woven into this rough tunic refract light into colorful needles.

    Let light fall upon form,
    As moons through ash.
    Let shadow fall upon the world,
    As seeds into soil.

Notes

  • This is the common shirt worn by the Urshilaku Ashlander tribe, most spiritual of the clans who roam Vvardenfell.
